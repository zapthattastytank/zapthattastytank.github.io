This is the first attempt at keeping the need and want for a storage system at bay. The floating wizards hut is being help up by the mage totems below. The totems desire fuel in the form of blaze rods.

Inside the tower this is the storage system which is really only a stack of chests with hoppers connecting the tower. Each tower is then connected to a light system which shows how full the chests are.
